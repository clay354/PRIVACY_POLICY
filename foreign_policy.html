1. What is Personal Information?

Personal information is any data that can identify you as an individual.
This includes your name, phone number, and records created while using our services.

3. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your personal information for the following purposes within the minimum necessary scope:

  • Member registration, service provision, customer support, and fraud prevention
  • Age verification and identity confirmation
  • Booking/purchase services and payment processing
  • User identification for personalized services
  • Service improvement and statistical analysis

4. How Do We Use Location Data?

"Find nearby accommodations" feature uses your location temporarily only when you use the map.

  • Purpose: Recommend accommodations and activities near your location
  • Retention: One-time use, deleted immediately (verification records kept for 1 year per law)

7. Cookies & Personalized Advertising

How to Block Cookies

You can protect your information by blocking cookies in your browser settings.

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data
  • iPhone: Settings > Safari > Block all cookies
Disable Personalized Advertising

If you don't want ads based on your interests, you can disable them in your device settings.

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID
  • iPhone: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Disable app tracking
